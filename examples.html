Examples from the same domain in real life

  • Hello In Elephant is an AI powered app and website that allows you to translate your own voice and conversation into elephant sounds.
  • The Orcasound web app allows people to listen to the sounds made by killer whales on the northeast Pacific coost in real time.
  • The NADA soundwalk focuses on how one can tune in and feel the sound of each element. For example, air is perceptible to ears and skin through sound and touch.
  • A Garden Through Time is a soundwalk available on the Echoes app that allow you to listen to an audio guide while walking on fifty specified routes in the UK.
  • Acoustic ecologist Gordon Hempton's Sound Tracker Project shares recordings of nature from around the world, and his quest to save silence from extinction.
  • Hiveopolis is a digital honeybee colony system where bees, humans and hives can interact.
